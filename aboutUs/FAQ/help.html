How to Sign Up


Navigate to the “My Account” page located in the navigation bar in the home page.
Enter the details required such as OSU ID, Username, Name, and Password.
Once the signup is successful, the web page should redirect you back to the main page where
the “Find a study space” feature will be found in the navigation bar.

How to Log In


Navigate to the “My Account” page located in the navigation bar in the home page.
Then, enter the correct username and password combination. If the sign up is successful, the web page
should redirect you back to the main page where the “Find a study space” feature
will be found in the navigation bar. Otherwise, you will be notified that the login was
unsuccessful and you can go back and try again.

How to make a Reservation


Once you are logged into your account, you are now able to make a reservation.
To do this, navigate to the “Find a Study Space” option on the navigation bar. On the page, you will see a
calendar for the month of December. Click on a day that you wish to make a reservation for.
Once clicked, a modal will pop up requesting the start and end times. Then, you may press reserve to
make the reservation.

How to edit a Reservation


To edit an existing reservation, navigate to the “Find a Study Space” option on
the navigation bar. On the page, you will see a list of your reservations under the calendar. Simply click the edit button next
to the reservation you wish to change, enter in the new information in the pop up, and the reservation
will be updated accordingly.

How to remove a Reservation


To removing an existing reservation, navigate to the “Find a Study Space” option on the navigation bar.
On the page, you will see a list of your reservations under the calendar. Simply click the remove button next to the
reservation you wish to change, and the reservation will be updated accordingly.
